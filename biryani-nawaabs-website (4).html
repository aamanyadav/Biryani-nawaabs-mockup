Our Story

Eight Centuries. One Recipe.

From a walled city on the Gomti to kitchens across the world — the journey of the dum.

1

Ancient Lucknow

A city of poets, courtyards and slow afternoons — where every meal was written like verse, and hospitality was law.

2

Royal Kitchens

Inside the Nawaabi bawarchikhana, master cooks guarded recipes passed only by whisper, mastered over a lifetime.

3

The Secret Dum Process

Sealed with dough, cooked on ember-slow heat — the dum technique locks in every drop of flavour, untouched for generations.

4

Heritage Recipes

Recipes once reserved for royal tables, carried forward exactly as they were — nothing shortened, nothing substituted.

5

Modern Restaurants

The same recipes, plated for today — restaurants built with warmth, craft and a sense of occasion.

6

Cloud Kitchens

Taking the royal dastarkhwan into every neighbourhood — fresh, fast, and just as authentic at your door.

7

Expansion Across India

From Lucknow outward — a growing family of outlets, each one carrying the same seal of the dum.

8

Future Global Brand

The next chapter: Biryani Nawaabs on the world's tables, still rooted in one kitchen in Lucknow.
